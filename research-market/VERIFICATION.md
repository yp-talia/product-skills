## Overall Assessment

The EXAMPLE.md is **broadly accurate on major facts** -- market sizes, funding amounts, regulatory details, and survey statistics are real and verifiable. However, three systemic issues undermine source integrity:

1. **Source misattribution (most common issue):** Multiple data points attributed to aiagentsdirectory.com that are not in the cited article. The Gartner market size figure is attributed to JetBrains. The aiagentsdirectory.com article is being used as a catch-all citation for agentic AI funding data it does not contain.

2. **Editorial inference presented as sourced:** The multi-model routing claim (GPT-4o for web, Grok for reasoning) is opinion dressed up with a source citation to aitoolanalysis.com, which does not support those specific claims.

3. **Precision issues:** The Tessl valuation is understated ($500M+ vs actual $750M). The Menlo Ventures spending trajectory omits the 2024 midpoint. GPAI documentation requirements are presented as mandatory when they are currently voluntary Code of Practice.

No claims appear to be entirely fabricated -- each has a kernel of verifiable truth -- but the sourcing discipline needs tightening, particularly around the aiagentsdirectory.com over-citation and the JetBrains/Gartner conflation.
